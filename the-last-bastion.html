About the Game

The Last Bastion is a tower defense game developed in just 10 days for the GameDev.tv Game Jam 2024. The project required rapid prototyping and asset creation under tight deadlines.


Key Contributions

  • 3D Asset Creation: Created 100% of the 3D art assets used in the game during the 10-day jam period.
  • Level Design: Designed the map layout to balance difficulty and tower placement strategies.
  • Special Effects: Developed custom particle systems and shaders to enhance visual feedback for attacks and destruction.
