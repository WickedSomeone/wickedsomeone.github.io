About the Game

Iron Echoes is a post-apocalyptic action RPG that tells the story of Zenith, a robotic wanderer who awakens amid the ruins left by The Great War. With corrupted data masking fragments of a lost past, Zenith sets off on a quest for answers, venturing through a world where every encounter compels exploration.


My Contributions

  • Modular Quest System: Developed a robust quest and dialogue system built for modularity, allowing designers to easily add new narrative content without touching code.
  • Enemy AI & Combat: Implemented basic enemy AI and audio systems. Built complex state machines to enable smooth transitions and nuanced behavior logic.
  • Boss Fight Design: Designed and developed the final boss mechanics, AI behavior, and combat flow for a challenging climax.
  • Visual Effects: Worked in Shader Graph to produce an outline shader that distinguishes selected characters, improving visual clarity.
  • Character Art: Created character assets to enrich the world's visual storytelling, compensating for limited artistic resources.
